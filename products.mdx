# Product Runway

This is the catalogue for everything in the Avi ecosystem. Each product ships with a 40-word story, a differentiator, and a “read next” path so you can dig deeper into the ideas powering it.

## Thinki.sh

Preventive thinking playbooks to spot problems before they surface. Frameworks, canvases, and interactive tools for founders and operators. Built in public with live workshops, Loom walkthroughs, and printable canvases.

- **Link**: [https://thinki.sh](https://thinki.sh)
- **Read next**: [Thinki.sh Preventive Playbooks](/introduction)

## MetaLabs.sh

A lab of AI-forward apps—design systems, agent pipelines, internal dev-tools—that make small teams feel enterprise-level without the drag. Every release includes architecture notes and component libraries.

- **Link**: [https://metalabs.sh](https://metalabs.sh)
- **Read next**: [AI Lab articles](/ai/generative-ai)

## Groc

A grocery-planning companion built for healthy families. Auto-generates lists, nutrition insights, and budget views powered by n8n automations, AirTable bases, and SMS nudges.

- **Link**: Coming soon
- **Read next**: [Productivity Life OS Lanes](/productivity/life-hacks)

## PromptLib

Curated prompt library for builders, writers, and educators. Browse by persona, tag, or domain and remix logic into your own agents. Includes evaluation notes and failure modes.

- **Link**: [https://promptlib.metalabs.sh](https://promptlib.metalabs.sh)
- **Read next**: [AI Lab / Prompt Craft](/ai/generative-ai)

## AI-Agent

A playground for composing autonomous agents with context awareness, planning stacks, and secure action layers. Ships with templates, observability hooks, and responsible-use guides.

- **Link**: [https://www.ai-agent.sh](https://www.ai-agent.sh)
- **Read next**: [Agent Diaries](/ai/generative-ai)

## GlucosePro

Health companion offering bite-sized guides on blood glucose, complete with calculators rooted in clinical research and personal experimentation.

- **Link**: [https://glucosepro.metalabs.sh](https://glucosepro.metalabs.sh)
- **Read next**: [Productivity Energy Stack](/productivity/health-organization)

## nishabdham.sh

Home to my Telugu poetry, essays, and cultural reflections. Expect bilingual storytelling, audio renditions, and behind-the-scenes notes on craft.

- **Link**: [https://nishabdham.sh](https://nishabdham.sh)
- **Read next**: [Nishabdham Verse Garden](https://nishabdham.sh)
